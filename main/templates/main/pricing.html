Transparent Billing

We do not plan on getting paid without our customer wanting us to. KnowHub is a bootstrapped company and we hope you will be eager to pay for our product because it makes your life easier and your business more efficient.

At any point you can visit your account's billing page and see the date and amount of the next charge. At the same page you can cancel your subscription.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try KnowHub for free?
Yes! You can sign up for KnowHub right now and get a 30 days free trial. We hope this will give you enough time to evaluate KnowHub for your company.
What happens if my trial expires and I want to cancel?
We do not plan on getting paid by accident. We will send you a reminder 3 days before your very first charge in case you want to cancel after your free trial. In addition, if asked, we will refund any subscription cycle that is in the first 3 days (out of the total 30).
How are users counted for billing?
You will be billed for the number of users who can log into KnowHub. Admins may deactivate users at any time.
What payment options are available?
We accept the following credit cards:
  • Visa
  • MasterCard
  • American Express
  • Diners Club
  • JCB
  • Discover
If these do not cover your needs feel free to contact us.
Can I get a refund?
Our refund policy is based on the use of our product. All refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Send us an email with the details of your experience and we’ll work with you to make sure you’re happy.
Is there a free plan beyond the trial?
Currently there isn't one. Are you interested? Tell us if so!
